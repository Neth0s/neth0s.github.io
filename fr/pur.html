Cassiopée Projects

Cassiopée Projects are 6 months research and development projects that are part of Télécom SudParis's 2nd year curriculum. My project is made of a team of 4 students whose goal is to test Unreal's capacities and limits as a game engine.

Projet Unreal Réel (working title)

To help us focus our creativity, our project's one constraint was to create a game inspired by this photo. We decided to go with a metroidvania where the player is a forest spirit tasked with healing a world tree growing in all kinds of directions with it's own gravity, like in the Mario Galaxy series.
