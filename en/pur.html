Juniper Jupiters

- A 2021 Cassiopée Project -

Cassiopée Projects

Cassiopée Projects are 6 months research and development projects that are part of Télécom SudParis's 2nd year curriculum. My project is made of a team of 5 students whose goal is to test Unreal's capacities and limits as a game engine.

Juniper Jupiters

To help us focus our creativity, our project's one constraint was to create a game inspired by this photo. We decided to go with a metroidvania where the player is a forest spirit tasked with healing a world tree growing in all kinds of directions with it's own gravity, like in the Mario Galaxy series.

Explore its interconnected and maze-like growths, from dark roots to bright canopies, using the peculiar gravity to your advantage. Fight against the maddened dwellers of the plant to free them from their strange frenzy, and learn new combat tricks and movement abilities from them.
