Shadow University Program

What is Shadow University Program (SUP)?

Shadow is a Cloud gaming service developed by the French company Blade. They provide a remote access to a complete PC infrastructure which notably allows executing video games remotely.

Their University Program supports student gaming clubs and associations and helps them realise their projects by assisting them, providing them with equipement and inviting them to gaming events.

A link to Shadow's blog post (in French) here.

My contribution to entering SUP

In order to be allowed in SUP, your club has to accomplish different "missions", gets points for each of them and level up, with awards for each level. One of these missions was creating a video game in the span of one week. With the help of another person, I created a small RPG of two students desperatly wanting to be part of SUP and breaking in the offices of Shadow.

Only 24 clubs succeded this mission, and among them our game was awarded 2nd place. This allowed our club to get Shadow equipement and have the club's room renovated by Shadow.
