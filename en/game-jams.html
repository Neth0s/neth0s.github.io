Game Jams

What's a Game Jam ?

Game Jams a short events (usually 48h to 72h) where participants try to make a video game from scratch that follows a specific theme. According to the format, people might enter independantly or in teams.

You can find all my game jam entries on my itch.io page here : nethos.itch.io

Celestial Debris - Unijam Winner 2021

Take a stroll on your planet littered with space trash, humanity's legacy to the galaxy. Use them to make your own spaceship and take off this planet to discover what happened to the human race though the different logs associated with each debris.

In this relaxing walking simulator on an alien planet with a rich and diverse vegetation, you uncover the story of humanity and learn how our disastrous waste management caused its downfall. The game was elected winner of Unijam 2021 by the public.

This game was a submission for Unijam 2021 for which the theme was "One man's trash is another man's treasure.". With this quote I really wanted to make a game about recycling and talk about our problem with garbage.

Bulgarian Umbrella - Unijam 2020

Armed with his poisonous umbrella, he takes out his targets with class and stealth. But he yet has to know who his target is...

In this puzzle game, you are given a target to kill, but you don't know their name or what they look like. You need to investigate to discover their identity otherwise you might kill the wrong person! It has been rewarded : Best graphics, a Jury's favorite.

This game was a submission for Unijam 2020 for which the theme was "With great power comes great responsibility."

Super Car Go! - Unijam 2019

The only racing game where you have to arrive last. But be careful, stopping is deadly!

This game was a submission for Unijam 2019 for which the theme was "Do not fear being slow, fear only being still.". Unijam is a yearly game jam organised by Télécom SudParis and ENSIIE that lasts about 40 hours.

Rush To Heaven - Sept. 2019

You just died, but if you don't want to spend the afterlife in hell. So you must go to heaven as fast as possible. But beware, there are a lot of obstacles on the way and crashing inadvertedly into any of them will mark the end of your afterlife.

This game was a submission for the Talkative Server World Wide Jam #1 for which the theme was Afterlife.

2D infinite scroller
Unity

Legend of The True Ninja - GMTK Game Jam 2019

You are the True Ninja, a master in the art of stealth. However, your enemies cursed you with a spell and you need to find your real self among the replicas, who try to follow your every moves.

This game was a submission for the GMTK Game Jam 2019 for which the theme was Only one.
